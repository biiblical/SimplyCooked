Bibimbap


Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 2 cups cooked short-grain white rice

  • 1 cup julienned carrots, sautéed

  • 1 cup thinly sliced cucumber

  • 1 cup bean sprouts, blanched

  • 1 cup sautéed spinach

  • 1 cup shiitake mushrooms, sliced and sautéed

  • 1 cup ground beef, seasoned and cooked

  • 4 fried eggs

  • Sesame oil for drizzling

  • Sesame seeds for garnish

  • Gochujang (Korean red pepper paste) for serving

Preparation

ASSEMBLE BIBIMBAP

    Step 1

  1. Divide the cooked rice among four bowls, creating a bed for the toppings.

    2. Step 2

  2. Arrange the julienned carrots, sliced cucumber, blanched bean sprouts, sautéed spinach, sautéed shiitake mushrooms, and seasoned ground beef in separate sections on top of the rice.

    3. Step 3

  3. Place a fried egg on the center of each bowl.

    4. Step 4

  4. Drizzle sesame oil over the ingredients and sprinkle sesame seeds on top for garnish.

    5. Step 5

  5. Serve Bibimbap with a side of Gochujang for those who want to add a spicy kick.

    6. Step 6

  6. Before eating, mix all the ingredients together, ensuring the egg yolk combines with the rice and other toppings.

    7. Step 7

  7. Enjoy your delicious Bibimbap!
