Fideua

Typical Valecian dish, but enjoyed all around Spain.


Ingredients (4 people)

  • 4 langoustines

  • 12 prawns

  • 400 grams of monkfish

  • 400 grams of fideos

  • 1 crushed tomato (150 g)

  • Vera dulce (sweet Paprika) (8-10 g)

  • Â½ a large onion, diced

  • 2 cloves of garlic

  • 2 eggs

  • 200 ml of extra virgin olive oil (approx)

  • Salt

  • 1.5 litres of fish stock

Preparation

    Step 1

  1. Add the prawns and the langoustines to the Paella Pan with the hot oil and cook until golden.

    2. Step 2

  2. Once golden, after 2-3 minutes of stirring, remove them from the pan.

    3. Step 3

  3. Add the diced onion and garlic and fry.

    4. Step 4

  4. Then add the monkfish and cook until golden.

    5. Step 5

  5. Add the crushed tomato and paprika and continue frying, be careful not to burn the paprika.

    6. Step 6

  6. Add the fideos and fry for 3 minutes so that the flavours from the other ingredients in the pan are absorbed by the fideos.

    7. Step 7

  7. Add 1 litre of stock. The ratio is around just over 2 parts stock to 1 part fideos.

    8. Step 8

  8. Cook for 10 minutes on high heat.

    9. Step 9

  9. Taste it and add salt if necessary.

    10. Step 10

  10. Arrange the prawns and langoustines on top of the pasta in a nice pattern.

    11. Step 11

  11. Turn the heat to medium and leave to cook for another 10 minutes. If it looks like the stock is being absorbed very quickly, add a little more.

    12. Step 12

  12. Take it off the heat and leave to rest uncovered for 5 minutes so that any excess stock can be absorbed.

When ready, it should be a smooth fideua and not look like soupy rice.
