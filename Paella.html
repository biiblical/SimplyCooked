Paella


Ingredients (10-12 crepes)

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour

  • 2 large eggs

  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar

  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

  • Additional butter for cooking

  • Favorite fillings and toppings (e.g., Nutella, fresh berries, whipped cream, maple syrup)

Preparation

    Step 1

  1. In a blender, combine flour, eggs, milk, melted butter, sugar, and salt. Blend until the batter is smooth. Let the batter rest in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.

    2. Step 2

  2. Heat a non-stick skillet or crepe pan over medium heat. Add a small amount of butter to coat the surface.

    3. Step 3

  3. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter into the center of the skillet, swirling it to spread evenly over the bottom. Cook for about 1-2 minutes or until the edges begin to brown.

    4. Step 4

  4. Flip the crepe using a spatula and cook the other side for an additional 1-2 minutes until golden brown.

    5. Step 5

  5. Repeat with the remaining batter, adding a small amount of butter to the skillet before each crepe.

    6. Step 6

  6. Once cooked, fill the crepes with your favorite fillings and toppings. Popular choices include Nutella, fresh berries, whipped cream, or maple syrup.

    7. Step 7

  7. Fold or roll the crepes and serve immediately. Enjoy your delicious Classic Crepes!
