Burger


Ingredients (4 burgers)

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef (80% lean)

  • Salt and black pepper to taste

  • 4 burger buns

  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)

  • Lettuce leaves

  • Sliced tomatoes

  • Sliced onions

  • Pickle slices

  • Ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise for condiments

  • Vegetable oil for grilling

Preparation

    Step 1

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop grill pan to medium-high heat.

    2. Step 2

  2. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into burger patties. Season both sides with salt and black pepper.

    3. Step 3

  3. Place the burger patties on the preheated grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side for medium doneness. Adjust the cooking time based on your desired level of doneness.

    4. Step 4

  4. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking and close the grill lid to melt the cheese.

    5. Step 5

  5. While the burgers are cooking, cut the burger buns in half and lightly toast them on the grill.

    6. Step 6

  6. Assemble the burgers: Place a burger patty on the bottom half of each bun. Top with lettuce leaves, sliced tomatoes, sliced onions, and pickle slices.

    7. Step 7

  7. Spread condiments such as ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise on the top half of the buns.

    8. Step 8

  8. Place the top bun over the toppings to complete the burgers.

    9. Step 9

  9. Serve your Classic Beef Burgers hot with your favorite sides. Enjoy!
