Naan


Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour

  • 1 teaspoon sugar

  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast

  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

  • 3/4 cup warm milk

  • 2 tablespoons yogurt

  • 2 tablespoons ghee or melted butter

  • Garlic, chopped (optional, for garlic naan)

  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (optional, for garnish)

Preparation

    Step 1

  1. In a bowl, combine warm milk, sugar, and active dry yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.

    2. Step 2

  2. In a large mixing bowl, sift together all-purpose flour and salt.

    3. Step 3

  3. Add the frothy yeast mixture, yogurt, and ghee or melted butter to the flour. Mix to form a soft dough.

    4. Step 4

  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.

    5. Step 5

  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours or until doubled in size.

    6. Step 6

  6. Punch down the risen dough and divide it into equal portions. Roll each portion into a ball.

    7. Step 7

  7. Preheat a cast-iron skillet or a non-stick pan over medium heat.

    8. Step 8

  8. Roll out each dough ball into a round or oval shape (naan) on a floured surface.

    9. Step 9

  9. Place the rolled-out dough onto the hot skillet and cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.

    10. Step 10

  10. For garlic naan, sprinkle chopped garlic on top before flipping.

    11. Step 11

  11. Brush the cooked naan with ghee or melted butter and garnish with chopped cilantro if desired.

    12. Step 12

  12. Repeat the process for the remaining dough portions.

    13. Step 13

  13. Serve Naan warm and enjoy with your favorite curries or dips!
