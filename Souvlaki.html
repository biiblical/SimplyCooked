Souvlaki


Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 1.5 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into cubes

  • 1/4 cup olive oil

  • 3 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme

  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin

  • 1 teaspoon paprika

  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder

  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional for heat)

  • Salt and pepper, to taste

  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice

  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Preparation

MARINATE THE CHICKEN

    Step 1

  1. In a bowl, combine olive oil, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, ground cumin, paprika, onion powder, cayenne pepper (if using), salt, and pepper. Mix well.

    2. Step 2

  2. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade, making sure each piece is well-coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes or up to 4 hours for better flavor.

    3. ASSEMBLE AND GRILL THE SOUVLAKI

    Step 1

  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.

    4. Step 2

  4. Thread the marinated chicken cubes onto the soaked wooden skewers.

    5. Step 3

  5. Brush the grill grates with oil to prevent sticking. Place the skewers on the grill.

    6. Step 4

  6. Grill the souvlaki for 12-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and has a nice char on the edges.

    7. Step 5

  7. During the last few minutes of grilling, brush the souvlaki with fresh lemon juice for added flavor.

    8. Step 6

  8. Remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.
