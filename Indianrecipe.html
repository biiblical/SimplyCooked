Chicken Tikka Masala

A classic Indian dish, chicken tikka masala is not as difficult to make as you may think. Packed with flavor, the creamy, spiced sauce covers tender pieces of chicken, just waiting to be served with homemade naan and perfect basmati rice.


Ingredients

  • 4 Cloves of garlic, grated

  • 1 tbsp. grated peeled fresh ginger

  • 2.5 tsp. garam masala

  • 2 tsp. ground coriander

  • 2 tsp. ground turmeric

  • 1.5 tsp. ground cumin

  • 1/3 cup plus 1 tbsp. of tomato paste, divided

  • Kosher salt

  • 1 cup of whole-milk yogurt

  • 2 lb. skinless, boneless chicken breasts

  • 2 tbsp. vegetable oil or ghee

  • 1 yellow onion, finely chopped

  • 1 to 3 fresh red chiles, seeded and finely chopped

  • 2.5 cups of tomato puree

  • 1 cup heavy cream

  • Half a cup of fresh, finely chopped cilantro leaves

  • Steamed basmati rice and naan, for serving


Preparation

    Step 1

  1. In a small bowl, mix garlic, ginger, garam masala, coriander, turmeric, cumin, 1 tablespoon tomato paste, and 1/4 teaspoon salt until a paste forms.

    2. Step 2

  2. In a medium bowl, stir yogurt, salt, and half of spice paste until well combined.

    3. Step 3

  3. Place chicken on a cutting board and make a few shallow cuts in each breast.

    4. Step 4

  4. Add to bowl and turn to coat. Cover and refrigerate at least 30 minutes and up to 6 hours.

    5. Step 5

  5. In a large pot over medium heat, heat oil.Add onion and chiles and cook, stirring often, until softened, 5 to 7 minutes.

    6. Step 6

  6. Add remaining 1/3 cup tomato paste and cook, stirring constantly, until darkened, 2 to 3 minutes. Add remaining spice paste and cook, stirring constantly, until fragrant, 2 to 3 minutes.

    7. Step 7

  7. Stir in tomato puree and 1/2 a cup of water, scraping up any browned bits in bottom of pot.

    8. Step 8

  8. Bring to a simmer and cook, stirring often and reducing heat to low if needed to maintain a gentle simmer, until mixture thickens, 10 to 12 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat broiler with an oven rack 6" from heat.

    9. Step 9

  9. Line a rimmed baking sheet with foil and place a wire rack inside sheet. Transfer chicken with marinade to rack and arrange in a single layer.

    10. Step 10

  10. Broil until chicken is mostly cooked and marinade has thickened and is golden in spots (chicken will not be fully cooked at this point), 10 to 12 minutes.

    11. Step 11

  11. Cut chicken into bite-size chunks, then add it and cilantro to tomato mixture.

    12. Step 12

  12. Very gently simmer, stirring occasionally, until chicken is cooked through, 5 to 8 minutes.

    13. Step 13

  13. Season with 1/2 teaspoon salt.

    14. When it's done, divide rice among bowls, spoon chicken tikki masala over, top with cilantro and serve with naan alongside.
