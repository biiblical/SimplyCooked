Dorayaki

Japanese Swee-Filled Pancakes


Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs

  • 2/3 cup sugar

  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda

  • 3 tablespoons water, or milk

  • 1 cup sifted all-purpose flour

  • Vegetable oil, for frying

  • 3/4 pound anko (sweet azuki bean paste)

Preparation

    Step 1

  1. Put eggs and sugar in a bowl and whisk very well.

    2. Step 2

  2. Dissolve baking soda in water.

    3. Step 3

  3. Add water into egg mixture.

    4. Step 4

  4. Add sifted flour into egg mixture gradually.

    5. Step 5

  5. Heat frying pan and lightly oil it.

    6. Step 6

  6. Pour a scoop of the batter into the frying pan and make a small pancake (about 3 to 4 inches in diameter).

    7. Step 7

  7. Turn over when bubbles appear on the surface.

    8. Step 8

  8. Repeat this process for each pancake.

    9. Step 9

  9. Cool the pancakes.

    10. Step 10

  10. Make pairs of pancakes, and put a scoop of anko sweet beans between them.
