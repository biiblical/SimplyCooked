Flammkuchen


Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 2 teaspoons active yeast (pizza yeast preferred)

  • 1/2 tablespoon sugar

  • 1 cup warm water

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour (or half rye flour, half all-purpose flour)

  • 1 teaspoon sea salt (or kosher)

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

  • 1 tablespoon oil (clarified butter or cooking)

  • 3 onions

  • 6 ounces smoked bacon (thick cut, not too fatty)

  • 7 ounces sour cream (or creme fraiche)

  • 1 large egg yolk

  • 1 teaspoon salt

  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper

  • 1 teaspoon nutmeg

Preparation

    Step 1

  1. In a large bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water and let sit for 5 minutes until frothy.

    2. Step 2

  2. Add the flour, salt, and olive oil to the bowl and mix until a dough forms.

    3. Step 3

  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.

    4. Step 4

  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp towel, and let rise in a warm place for 1 hour.

    5. Step 5

  5. Preheat your oven to 500°F (260°C) and place a pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven to heat up.

    6. Step 6

  6. In a skillet, cook the bacon until crispy and set aside.

    7. Step 7

  7. In the same skillet, cook the onions until caramelized and set aside.

    8. Step 8

  8. In a small bowl, mix together the sour cream, egg yolk, salt, pepper, nutmeg, and caraway seeds (if using).

    9. Step 9

  9. Roll out the dough on a floured surface until it is very thin and transfer it to a piece of parchment paper.

    10. Step 10

  10. Spread the sour cream mixture over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

    11. Step 11

  11. Top the sour cream mixture with the caramelized onions and bacon.

    12. Step 12

  12. Brush the edges of the dough with clarified butter or oil.

    13. Step 13

  13. Slide the parchment paper with the Flammkuchen onto the hot pizza stone or baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes until the edges are crispy and the toppings are golden brown.
