Gyros


Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped

  • 14 ounces ground lamb or beef (for Gyros)

  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

  • 3/4 cup beef or lamb stock

  • 2 tablespoons Greek yogurt (optional)

  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar

  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

  • 4 pita bread rounds

  • 1 large tomato, diced

  • 1 cucumber, sliced

  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced

  • Tzatziki sauce, for serving

Preparation

MAKE THE FILLING

    Step 1

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add 1 medium finely chopped onion and saut√© for 3 to 4 minutes or until soft and translucent.

    2. Step 2

  2. Add 14 ounces (400 grams) ground lamb or beef and cook for 3 to 4 minutes, stirring and breaking up with a wooden spoon until browned. If there is excess fat, you can drain it.

    3. Step 3

  3. In a small container, whisk together 1 tablespoon cornstarch and 1 tablespoon of the beef or lamb stock and set aside.

    4. Step 4

  4. Add remaining beef or lamb stock, Greek yogurt (if using), 1/8 cup red wine vinegar, and dried oregano to the ground meat in the skillet. Stir well to combine.

    5. Step 5

  5. Add the reserved cornstarch-water mixture and stir. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low and simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes or until thickened. Remove from heat and cool.

    6. ASSEMBLE THE GYROS

    Step 1

  6. Warm the pita bread rounds according to package instructions.

    7. Step 2

  7. Spread a generous spoonful of the cooled meat mixture onto each pita bread round.

    8. Step 3

  8. Top with diced tomatoes, cucumber slices, and thinly sliced red onion.

    9. Step 4

  9. Drizzle with tzatziki sauce.

    10. Step 5

  10. Fold the pita in half and serve immediately.
