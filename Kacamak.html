Kacamak


Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 2 cups cornmeal

  • 4 cups water

  • 1 teaspoon salt

  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled

  • 1/4 cup butter

  • Additional feta cheese for serving (optional)

Preparation

MAKE THE KACAMAK

    Step 1

  1. In a large pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil.

    2. Step 2

  2. Gradually whisk in 2 cups of cornmeal, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.

    3. Step 3

  3. Add salt to the mixture and continue stirring over medium heat until the mixture thickens and the cornmeal is fully cooked. This typically takes about 10-15 minutes.

    4. Step 4

  4. Stir in crumbled feta cheese and butter until well combined.

    5. Step 5

  5. Remove the pot from heat and let it rest for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

    6. SERVE AND ENJOY

    Step 1

  6. Serve the Kacamak hot, either as a creamy porridge or molded onto plates.

    7. Step 2

  7. Optionally, top with additional crumbled feta cheese for extra richness.

    8. Step 3

  8. Enjoy your comforting and flavorful Kacamak!
