Baklava


Ingredients

  • 1 pound (about 4 cups) phyllo dough, thawed if frozen

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted

  • 2 cups walnuts or almonds, finely chopped

  • 1 cup granulated sugar

  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves

  • 1 cup water

  • 1 cup granulated sugar (for syrup)

  • 1/2 cup honey

  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

Preparation

ASSEMBLE THE BAKLAVA

    Step 1

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C).

    2. Step 2

  2. In a bowl, combine the finely chopped nuts, 1 cup of sugar, ground cinnamon, and ground cloves.

    3. Step 3

  3. Brush a 9x13-inch baking dish with melted butter.

    4. Step 4

  4. Place a sheet of phyllo dough in the dish, brush it with melted butter, and repeat until you have about 8 layers.

    5. Step 5

  5. Sprinkle a portion of the nut mixture over the phyllo layers.

    6. Step 6

  6. Continue layering phyllo and nuts, brushing each layer with butter, until you run out of nut mixture.

    7. Step 7

  7. Finish with a top layer of phyllo, ensuring to brush the top generously with melted butter.

    8. BAKE AND MAKE SYRUP

    Step 1

  8. Cut the baklava into diamond or square shapes before baking.

    9. Step 2

  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 50-60 minutes, or until golden brown and crisp.

    10. Step 3

  10. While the baklava is baking, prepare the syrup. In a saucepan, combine 1 cup of sugar, honey, water, and lemon juice. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes until slightly thickened.

    11. Step 4

  11. Once the baklava is done baking, immediately pour the hot syrup over the hot baklava. Allow it to absorb the syrup and cool completely in the pan.

    12. Step 5

  12. Once cooled, carefully remove the individual pieces and serve.

    13. Step 6

  13. Enjoy your delicious homemade Baklava!
