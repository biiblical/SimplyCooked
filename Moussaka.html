Moussaka


Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • 1 large eggplant, sliced into rounds

  • 1 pound ground lamb or beef

  • 1 onion, finely chopped

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes

  • 1/4 cup tomato paste

  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)

  • Salt and pepper, to taste

  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

  • 1 cup milk

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

  • 1 large egg, beaten

Preparation

MAKE THE FILLING

    Step 1

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).

    2. Step 2

  2. Brush eggplant slices with olive oil and arrange on a baking sheet. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until softened and lightly browned. Set aside.

    3. Step 3

  3. In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, sauté until softened.

    4. Step 4

  4. Add ground lamb or beef to the skillet, cook until browned. Drain excess fat if necessary.

    5. Step 5

  5. Stir in dried oregano, ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and red wine (if using). Season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until the mixture thickens. Remove from heat.

    6. ASSEMBLE AND BAKE THE MOUSSAKA

    Step 1

  6. In a greased baking dish, layer half of the eggplant slices at the bottom.

    7. Step 2

  7. Spread the meat mixture over the eggplant layer. Top with the remaining eggplant slices.

    8. Step 3

  8. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in flour to make a roux. Gradually whisk in milk until the mixture thickens. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.

    9. Step 4

  9. Whisk in beaten egg and grated Parmesan cheese into the milk mixture. Pour the béchamel sauce over the eggplant and meat layers.

    10. Step 5

  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 40-45 minutes or until the top is golden brown and the Moussaka is cooked through. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
