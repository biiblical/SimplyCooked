Cevapcici


Ingredients (Makes about 20 Cevapcici)

  • 1 pound ground beef

  • 1/2 pound ground pork

  • 1 small onion, finely grated

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 tablespoon sweet paprika

  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin

  • 1 teaspoon baking soda

  • Salt and black pepper to taste

  • Vegetable oil (for greasing and grilling)

  • Flatbread or pita (for serving)

  • Chopped onions, chopped tomatoes, and yogurt (optional, for serving)

Preparation

MAKE THE CEVAPCICI MIXTURE AND GRILL

    Step 1

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, ground pork, finely grated onion, minced garlic, sweet paprika, ground cumin, baking soda, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all ingredients are evenly combined.

    2. Step 2

  2. Shape the mixture into small, finger-sized sausages (cevapcici).

    3. Step 3

  3. Preheat the grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Lightly grease the grates with vegetable oil.

    4. Step 4

  4. Grill the cevapcici for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are browned and cooked through.

    5. Step 5

  5. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes.

    6. SERVE AND ENJOY

    Step 1

  6. Serve the cevapcici on flatbread or pita.

    7. Step 2

  7. Optionally, garnish with chopped onions and tomatoes. Serve with yogurt on the side.

    8. Step 3

  8. Enjoy your delicious homemade cevapcici!
