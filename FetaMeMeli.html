Feta Me Meli


Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 1/2 pound feta cheese, cut into rectangles or cubes

  • Phyllo pastry sheets, thawed

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

  • 1/4 cup honey

  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts or pistachios (optional)

  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

  • Powdered sugar, for dusting (optional)

Preparation

ASSEMBLE AND BAKE FETA ME MELI

    Step 1

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).

    2. Step 2

  2. Place one sheet of phyllo pastry on a clean surface and brush it lightly with melted butter. Add another sheet on top and repeat until you have 4-5 layers.

    3. Step 3

  3. Place a piece of feta cheese in the center of the phyllo layers. Optionally, sprinkle chopped nuts over the feta.

    4. Step 4

  4. Drizzle honey over the feta and nuts. Sprinkle ground cinnamon on top.

    5. Step 5

  5. Wrap the phyllo pastry around the feta to form a neat parcel. Repeat for additional servings.

    6. Step 6

  6. Place the wrapped feta parcels on a baking sheet, seam side down. Brush the tops with melted butter.

    7. Step 7

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until the phyllo is golden brown and crispy.

    8. Step 8

  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.

    9. Step 9

  9. Optional: Dust with powdered sugar before serving.
