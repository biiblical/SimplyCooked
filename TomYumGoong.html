Tom Yum Goong


Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 1 lb (450g) large shrimp, peeled and deveined

  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth

  • 2 lemongrass stalks, bruised and cut into 2-inch pieces

  • 3 kaffir lime leaves, torn into pieces

  • 3-4 Thai bird's eye chilies, smashed (adjust to taste)

  • 3 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 medium-sized tomato, cut into wedges

  • 1 small onion, sliced

  • 200g button mushrooms, sliced

  • 1 tablespoon galangal or ginger, sliced

  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce

  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce

  • 1 tablespoon sugar

  • 1/4 cup lime juice

  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Preparation

    Step 1

  1. In a pot, bring chicken or vegetable broth to a boil. Add lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, Thai bird's eye chilies, and galangal or ginger. Simmer for 10-15 minutes to infuse flavors.

    2. Step 2

  2. Add sliced onions, minced garlic, and mushrooms to the pot. Cook for 3-5 minutes until the vegetables are tender.

    3. Step 3

  3. Stir in fish sauce, soy sauce, and sugar. Adjust the seasoning to your taste.

    4. Step 4

  4. Add shrimp and cook until they turn pink and opaque.

    5. Step 5

  5. Finally, add tomato wedges and lime juice. Simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes.

    6. Step 6

  6. Remove lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, and galangal slices before serving.

    7. Step 7

  7. Divide the Tom Yum soup into bowls, garnish with fresh cilantro leaves, and serve hot.

    8. Step 8

  8. Enjoy your delicious homemade Tom Yum Goong!
