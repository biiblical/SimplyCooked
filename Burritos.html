Burritos


Ingredients (Makes 4 Burritos)

  • 4 large flour tortillas

  • 1 pound ground beef or your choice of protein (chicken, pork, tofu)

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

  • 1 small onion, finely chopped

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 packet taco seasoning mix

  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, drained and rinsed

  • 1 cup cooked rice

  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or Mexican blend)

  • 1 cup salsa

  • 1 cup shredded lettuce

  • 1 avocado, sliced

  • Sour cream (for serving)

  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Preparation

COOK THE FILLING AND ASSEMBLE BURRITOS

    Step 1

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add chopped onions and cook until softened.

    2. Step 2

  2. Add minced garlic and cook for an additional 30 seconds until fragrant.

    3. Step 3

  3. Add ground beef (or your choice of protein) to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat if necessary.

    4. Step 4

  4. Stir in the taco seasoning mix and follow the package instructions for seasoning the meat.

    5. Step 5

  5. Add black beans to the skillet and cook until heated through.

    6. Step 6

  6. Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave according to package instructions.

    7. Step 7

  7. Assemble the burritos by placing a portion of the seasoned meat and beans mixture onto each tortilla.

    8. Step 8

  8. Top with cooked rice, shredded cheese, salsa, shredded lettuce, sliced avocado, sour cream, and chopped cilantro.

    9. Step 9

  9. Fold in the sides of the tortilla and then roll it up tightly to form the burrito.

    10. Step 10

  10. Serve the burritos immediately and enjoy!
