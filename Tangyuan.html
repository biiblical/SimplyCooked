Tangyuan


Ingredients (Makes about 20 Tangyuan)

  • 1 cup glutinous rice flour

  • Water (for making the dough)

  • Filling options: sweet red bean paste, sesame paste, or crushed peanuts mixed with sugar

  • Food coloring (optional)

  • 1/4 cup sesame seeds (for coating, optional)

Preparation

MAKE THE DOUGH AND ASSEMBLE TANGYUAN

    Step 1

  1. In a bowl, combine glutinous rice flour with water gradually, stirring continuously, until a smooth and elastic dough forms. Adjust the water as needed to achieve the right consistency.

    2. Step 2

  2. If desired, add a few drops of food coloring to the dough to create colorful Tangyuan. Knead until the color is evenly distributed.

    3. Step 3

  3. Divide the dough into small portions and flatten each portion into a round disc.

    4. Step 4

  4. Place a small amount of your chosen filling (sweet red bean paste, sesame paste, or crushed peanuts mixed with sugar) in the center of each disc.

    5. Step 5

  5. Wrap the dough around the filling, sealing the edges to form a smooth ball. Make sure there are no cracks.

    6. Step 6

  6. Repeat the process until all Tangyuan are assembled.

    7. Step 7

  7. If desired, roll the Tangyuan in sesame seeds for coating.

    8. Step 8

  8. Bring a pot of water to boil. Drop the Tangyuan into the boiling water and cook until they float to the surface, indicating that they are cooked.

    9. Step 9

  9. Remove the Tangyuan with a slotted spoon and serve them in a bowl.
