Om Ali


Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 4 cups puff pastry, torn into small pieces

  • 1/2 cup mixed nuts (almonds, pistachios, and hazelnuts), chopped

  • 1/4 cup raisins

  • 1/4 cup desiccated coconut

  • 4 cups whole milk

  • 1 cup heavy cream

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

  • Pinch of ground cinnamon for garnish

  • Pinch of shredded coconut for garnish

Preparation

    Step 1

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).

    2. Step 2

  2. Spread the torn puff pastry pieces on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven until golden and crispy. Remove from the oven and set aside.

    3. Step 3

  3. In a saucepan, heat the whole milk, heavy cream, and granulated sugar over medium heat. Stir until the sugar is dissolved and the mixture is warm but not boiling.

    4. Step 4

  4. Stir in the vanilla extract and remove the saucepan from heat.

    5. Step 5

  5. In a serving dish, layer half of the baked puff pastry pieces. Sprinkle half of the chopped nuts, raisins, and desiccated coconut over the pastry.

    6. Step 6

  6. Pour half of the warm milk mixture over the layers in the dish.

    7. Step 7

  7. Repeat the layering process with the remaining puff pastry, nuts, raisins, coconut, and milk mixture.

    8. Step 8

  8. Let the dessert sit for a few minutes, allowing the puff pastry to absorb the liquid.

    9. Step 9

  9. Garnish with a pinch of ground cinnamon and shredded coconut.

    10. Step 10

  10. Serve Om Ali warm and enjoy!
