Molokhia


Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 2 cups fresh or frozen Molokhia leaves (Jute leaves)

  • 1 lb (about 450g) chicken, lamb, or beef, cut into pieces

  • 1 large onion, finely chopped

  • 4 cloves garlic, minced

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil or vegetable oil

  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander

  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin

  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander

  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • 6 cups chicken or vegetable broth

  • Cooked rice or flatbread for serving

  • Lemon wedges for serving

Preparation

    Step 1

  1. If using fresh Molokhia leaves, wash and finely chop them. If using frozen Molokhia, thaw according to package instructions.

    2. Step 2

  2. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.

    3. Step 3

  3. Add minced garlic and sauté for another minute until fragrant.

    4. Step 4

  4. Add the meat pieces to the pot and brown on all sides.

    5. Step 5

  5. Season the meat with ground coriander, cumin, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Mix well to coat the meat with the spices.

    6. Step 6

  6. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 30-40 minutes or until the meat is tender.

    7. Step 7

  7. Add the chopped Molokhia leaves to the pot and simmer for an additional 15-20 minutes until the Molokhia is cooked and flavors are well combined.

    8. Step 8

  8. Adjust seasoning if necessary.

    9. Step 9

  9. Serve Molokhia over cooked rice or with flatbread. Garnish with lemon wedges.

    10. Step 10

  10. Enjoy your delicious Molokhia!
