Tacos


Ingredients (Makes 8 Tacos)

  • 8 small corn or flour tortillas

  • 1 pound ground beef or your choice of protein (chicken, pork, tofu)

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

  • 1 small onion, finely chopped

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 packet taco seasoning mix

  • 1 cup shredded lettuce

  • 1 cup diced tomatoes

  • 1 cup shredded cheddar or Mexican blend cheese

  • 1/2 cup sour cream

  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

  • Lime wedges (for serving)

  • Hot sauce (optional)

Preparation

COOK THE FILLING AND ASSEMBLE TACOS

    Step 1

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add chopped onions and cook until softened.

    2. Step 2

  2. Add minced garlic and cook for an additional 30 seconds until fragrant.

    3. Step 3

  3. Add ground beef (or your choice of protein) to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat if necessary.

    4. Step 4

  4. Stir in the taco seasoning mix and follow the package instructions for seasoning the meat.

    5. Step 5

  5. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave according to package instructions.

    6. Step 6

  6. Assemble the tacos by placing a spoonful of the seasoned meat onto each tortilla.

    7. Step 7

  7. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and shredded cheese.

    8. Step 8

  8. Drizzle with sour cream, sprinkle with chopped cilantro, and squeeze lime wedges over the top.

    9. Step 9

  9. Optionally, add hot sauce for extra heat.

    10. Step 10

  10. Serve the tacos immediately and enjoy!
