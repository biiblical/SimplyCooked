Buffalo Chicken Wings


Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 2 lbs (about 1 kg) chicken wings, split at joints, tips discarded

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour

  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder

  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika

  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted

  • 1/4 cup hot sauce (like Frank's RedHot)

  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar

  • 1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

  • Carrot and celery sticks for serving

  • Blue cheese dressing for dipping

Preparation

    Step 1

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

    2. Step 2

  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper.

    3. Step 3

  3. Dredge each chicken wing in the flour mixture, shaking off excess, and place them on the prepared baking sheet.

    4. Step 4

  4. Bake in the preheated oven for about 45-50 minutes or until the wings are golden brown and crispy.

    5. Step 5

  5. While the wings are baking, prepare the buffalo sauce. In a saucepan, melt butter, then stir in hot sauce, white vinegar, and Worcestershire sauce. Simmer for a few minutes, then remove from heat.

    6. Step 6

  6. Once the wings are done, transfer them to a large bowl. Pour the buffalo sauce over the wings and toss until evenly coated.

    7. Step 7

  7. Serve the Buffalo Chicken Wings hot with carrot and celery sticks and blue cheese dressing for dipping.

    8. Step 8

  8. Enjoy your delicious homemade Buffalo Chicken Wings!
