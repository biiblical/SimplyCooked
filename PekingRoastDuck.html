Peking Roast Duck


Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 1 whole Peking duck (about 4-5 pounds)

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce

  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce

  • 1 tablespoon Shaoxing wine or dry sherry

  • 1 teaspoon five-spice powder

  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

  • 2 tablespoons honey

  • 4 green onions, sliced

  • 1 cucumber, julienned

  • 1 package of Peking duck pancakes (available at Asian grocery stores)

  • Hoisin sauce, for serving

Preparation

MARINATE AND ROAST THE DUCK

    Step 1

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).

    2. Step 2

  2. Rinse the duck inside and out, pat dry with paper towels. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, hoisin sauce, Shaoxing wine, five-spice powder, sesame oil, and honey to create the marinade.

    3. Step 3

  3. Brush the marinade over the duck, ensuring it is well-coated inside and out. Let it marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight in the refrigerator.

    4. Step 4

  4. Place the duck on a roasting rack in a roasting pan. Roast in the preheated oven for 2 to 2.5 hours, or until the skin is crispy and golden brown.

    5. ASSEMBLE PEKING ROAST DUCK

    Step 1

  5. Remove the duck from the oven and let it rest for 15 minutes before carving.

    6. Step 2

  6. While the duck is resting, warm the Peking duck pancakes according to package instructions.

    7. Step 3

  7. Carve the duck into thin slices, including both skin and meat.

    8. Step 4

  8. Spread a thin layer of hoisin sauce onto each pancake.

    9. Step 5

  9. Place slices of duck, green onions, and julienned cucumber onto each pancake.

    10. Step 6

  10. Roll up the pancake, enclosing the fillings. Serve immediately.
