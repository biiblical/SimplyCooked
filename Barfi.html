Barfi


Ingredients (20 pieces)

  • 2 cups full-fat milk powder

  • 1 cup condensed milk

  • 1/2 cup ghee (clarified butter)

  • 1/2 cup milk

  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios, cashews), toasted

  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder

  • Edible silver foil for garnish (optional)

Preparation

    Step 1

  1. In a non-stick pan, heat ghee over medium heat.

    2. Step 2

  2. Add milk powder to the pan and roast it on low to medium heat until it turns light golden brown. Keep stirring to avoid burning.

    3. Step 3

  3. Once the milk powder is roasted, add condensed milk and continue stirring to combine well.

    4. Step 4

  4. Gradually add milk while stirring continuously to avoid lumps. Continue cooking until the mixture thickens and starts to leave the sides of the pan.

    5. Step 5

  5. Stir in cardamom powder and chopped toasted nuts. Mix well.

    6. Step 6

  6. Grease a square or rectangular pan with ghee and transfer the mixture into it. Smooth the top with a spatula.

    7. Step 7

  7. Allow the mixture to cool for a while, then refrigerate for 2-3 hours or until it sets completely.

    8. Step 8

  8. Once set, cut the Barfi into square or diamond shapes.

    9. Step 9

  9. Garnish with edible silver foil if desired.

    10. Step 10

  10. Serve and enjoy your delicious homemade Barfi!
