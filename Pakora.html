Pakora


Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 1 cup chickpea flour (besan)

  • 1/4 cup rice flour

  • 1 large potato, thinly sliced

  • 1 onion, thinly sliced

  • 1 cup spinach leaves, chopped

  • 1/2 cup cauliflower florets

  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated

  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds

  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder

  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)

  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda

  • Salt to taste

  • Water, as needed

  • Vegetable oil for deep frying

Preparation

    Step 1

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine chickpea flour, rice flour, cumin seeds, turmeric powder, red chili powder, baking soda, and salt.

    2. Step 2

  2. Add enough water to make a thick, smooth batter. Stir well to ensure there are no lumps.

    3. Step 3

  3. Add grated ginger to the batter and mix again.

    4. Step 4

  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.

    5. Step 5

  5. Dip potato slices, onion slices, spinach leaves, and cauliflower florets into the batter, ensuring they are well coated.

    6. Step 6

  6. Carefully drop the coated vegetables into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy.

    7. Step 7

  7. Use a slotted spoon to remove the pakoras from the oil and place them on a plate lined with paper towels to absorb excess oil.

    8. Step 8

  8. Repeat the process for the remaining batter and vegetables.

    9. Step 9

  9. Serve Pakoras hot with chutney or sauce of your choice.

    10. Step 10

  10. Enjoy your delicious homemade Pakoras!
