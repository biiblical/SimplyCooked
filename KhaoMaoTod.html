Khao Mao Tod


Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 2 cups sticky rice, soaked for 4-6 hours or overnight

  • 4 ripe bananas, peeled and cut into halves

  • 1 cup shredded coconut

  • 1/2 cup sesame seeds

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour

  • 1/2 cup rice flour

  • 1/4 cup sugar

  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

  • Vegetable oil for frying

Preparation

    Step 1

  1. Drain the soaked sticky rice and blend it until it becomes a coarse paste. Set aside.

    2. Step 2

  2. In a bowl, mix shredded coconut and sesame seeds. Set aside for coating.

    3. Step 3

  3. In another bowl, combine all-purpose flour, rice flour, sugar, and salt. Add water gradually to make a smooth batter.

    4. Step 4

  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or a heavy-bottomed pan to 350°F (175°C).

    5. Step 5

  5. Dip each banana half into the sticky rice paste, ensuring it's well-coated.

    6. Step 6

  6. Roll the coated banana in the coconut-sesame mixture, pressing gently to adhere.

    7. Step 7

  7. Dip the coated banana into the batter, covering it completely.

    8. Step 8

  8. Deep fry the banana until it turns golden brown and crispy. Ensure the sticky rice is cooked through.

    9. Step 9

  9. Remove from the oil and let it drain on paper towels.

    10. Step 10

  10. Repeat the process for the remaining banana halves.

    11. Step 11

  11. Serve Khao Mao Tod warm as a delightful snack or dessert.

    12. Step 12

  12. Enjoy your delicious homemade Khao Mao Tod!
