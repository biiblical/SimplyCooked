Steak Tartare


Ingredients

  • 8 ounces sirloin, beef tenderloin (filet mignon) or top round steak, fresh

  • 1 baguette, thinly sliced on a bias

  • ¼ cup olive oil or melted butter

  • 2 tablespoons capers, drained and finely chopped

  • 2 tablespoons cornichons, drained and finely chopped

  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh parsley leaves and tender stems, plus more for garnish

  • 1 small shallot, finely chopped

  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt, plus more to taste

  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, plus more to taste

  • 3 egg yolks, divided

  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce

  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice, freshly squeezed

Preparation

    Step 1

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit with a rack in the upper third..

    2. Step 2

  2. On a cutting board, freeze the beef until it is firm on the outside but still easily pierced with a knife, about 40 minutes.

    3. Step 3

  3. Spread baguette slices in a single layer on a rimmed baking sheet.

    4. Step 4

  4. Drizzle the slices with olive oil or butter and sprinkle with salt and pepper.

    5. Step 5

  5. Bake on the upper third oven rack until crispy and golden brown, 10–15 minutes (watch closely, as ovens vary).

    6. Step 6

  6. In a medium mixing bowl, whisk together the capers, cornichons, parsley, shallot, ½ teaspoon salt, ¼ teaspoon pepper, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, lemon juice, and 1 egg yolk.

    7. Step 7

  7. Using a sharp knife, thinly slice the steak against the grain, then cut slices into thin strips (¼-inch or thinner), then cut strips into a fine dice.

    8. Step 8

  8. Transfer the diced beef to the bowl with sauce and mix in to evenly coat. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

    9. Step 4

  9. Press base pastry into pie tins and press up the sides. Fill with cooled meat mixture. Brush rims with water.

    10. Step 9

  10. On a serving plate, pat half of the beef into a round cookie cutter or ramekin. Repeat on a second plate with the remaining half of the beef tartare.

    11. Step 10

  11. Using the back of a spoon, create a shallow well in the center of each steak tartare.

    12. Step 11

  12. Place a raw egg yolk in the well of each steak tartare.

    13. Step 12

  13. Serve immediately with toasted bread, and garnish with extra chopped parsley.
