Koshary


Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 1 cup dried lentils, rinsed

  • 1 cup rice

  • 1 cup elbow macaroni

  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas

  • 1 cup tomato sauce

  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced

  • 3 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1/4 cup white vinegar

  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin

  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

  • Chili sauce or hot sauce (optional)

Preparation

    Step 1

  1. In separate pots, cook the lentils, rice, and macaroni according to package instructions. Drain each and set aside.

    2. Step 2

  2. In a skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown and crispy. Remove half for garnish.

    3. Step 3

  3. To the remaining onions in the skillet, add minced garlic, ground cumin, and ground coriander. Saut√© for a couple of minutes until fragrant.

    4. Step 4

  4. Add tomato sauce to the skillet, and simmer for 5-7 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

    5. Step 5

  5. In a small bowl, mix white vinegar with the reserved crispy onions.

    6. Step 6

  6. To serve, layer the cooked lentils, rice, and macaroni on a plate. Top with cooked chickpeas.

    7. Step 7

  7. Pour the tomato sauce mixture over the layers.

    8. Step 8

  8. Drizzle the white vinegar and crispy onion mixture over the top.

    9. Step 9

  9. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley and, if desired, add chili sauce or hot sauce for extra heat.

    10. Step 10

  10. Serve your flavorful Koshary and enjoy!
