Barramundi


Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 4 Barramundi fillets

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest

  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice

  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme

  • Salt and black pepper to taste

  • Lemon wedges for serving

  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Preparation

    Step 1

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

    2. Step 2

  2. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper to create a marinade.

    3. Step 3

  3. Pat the Barramundi fillets dry and brush both sides with the marinade, ensuring they are well-coated.

    4. Step 4

  4. Let the fillets marinate for about 15-30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.

    5. Step 5

  5. Place the marinated Barramundi fillets on the preheated grill. Grill for about 4-5 minutes per side or until the fish is opaque and easily flakes with a fork.

    6. Step 6

  6. Once cooked, remove the fillets from the grill.

    7. Step 7

  7. Garnish the grilled Barramundi with lemon wedges and fresh parsley.

    8. Step 8

  8. Serve your Grilled Barramundi with your favorite sides and enjoy!
