Spring Rolls


Ingredients (Makes about 12 spring rolls)

  • 12 spring roll wrappers (rice paper)

  • 1 cup cooked and shredded chicken or tofu (for vegetarian)

  • 1 cup vermicelli rice noodles, cooked and drained

  • 1 cup shredded cabbage

  • 1 cup julienned carrots

  • 1 cup bean sprouts

  • Fresh mint leaves

  • Fresh cilantro leaves

  • Dipping sauce (hoisin sauce, peanut sauce, or soy sauce)

Preparation

ASSEMBLE SPRING ROLLS

    Step 1

  1. Prepare all the ingredients and have them ready for assembly.

    2. Step 2

  2. Fill a large bowl with warm water. Dip one spring roll wrapper into the water for a few seconds until it softens.

    3. Step 3

  3. Place the softened wrapper on a clean surface. In the center of the wrapper, add a small amount of shredded chicken or tofu, vermicelli rice noodles, shredded cabbage, julienned carrots, bean sprouts, mint leaves, and cilantro leaves.

    4. Step 4

  4. Roll the wrapper tightly over the filling, folding in the sides as you go, to form a neat and compact roll.

    5. Step 5

  5. Repeat the process until all the ingredients are used.

    6. Step 6

  6. Serve the spring rolls immediately, or cover them with a damp cloth to prevent drying out while you assemble the rest.

    7. Step 7

  7. Serve the spring rolls with your choice of dipping sauce.
