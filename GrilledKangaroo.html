Grilled Kangaroo


Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 1 lb (about 450g) kangaroo steaks

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme

  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary

  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika

  • Salt and black pepper to taste

  • 1 lemon, sliced for garnish

  • Fresh herbs (parsley, thyme, rosemary) for garnish

Preparation

    Step 1

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

    2. Step 2

  2. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, dried thyme, dried rosemary, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper to create a marinade.

    3. Step 3

  3. Brush the kangaroo steaks with the marinade, ensuring they are well-coated on both sides.

    4. Step 4

  4. Let the steaks marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.

    5. Step 5

  5. Place the marinated kangaroo steaks on the preheated grill. Grill for 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare doneness or adjust based on your preference.

    6. Step 6

  6. Once cooked, remove the steaks from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before slicing.

    7. Step 7

  7. Garnish the grilled kangaroo steaks with lemon slices and fresh herbs.

    8. Step 8

  8. Serve your Grilled Kangaroo steaks with your favorite sides and enjoy!
